Perfect selfie

Set your own custom beauty settings for facial enhancement and beautifying effects. These settings will be automatically applied when you take a groufie or selfie in Beauty mode.

  • On the home screen, touch Camera.
  • Touch the menu icon.
  • Touch Settings.
  • Touch Perfect selfie.
  • Enable Perfect selfie.
  • Touch Edit personal info.
  • First, take a front photo of yourself.
  • Next, take a side photo of yourself.
  • Finally, take a photo of yourself looking down.
  • On the Set beauty effects screen, choose the desired effects, such as a thin face, eye enlargement, or eye brightening.
  • Touch the check mark to save your settings.
  • In Beauty mode, touch the shutter icon to take a photo.
  • Touch the preview icon to preview the selected beauty effects.

Done.

Wi-Fi+

If you're having trouble accessing the Internet, turn on Wi-Fi+ to enable your phone to automatically connect to the network with the strongest signal (including both Wi-Fi and mobile data networks).

  • On the home screen, touch Settings.
  • On the All tab, touch More under Wireless & networks.
  • Touch LINK+.
  • Touch Wi-Fi+.
  • Touch Wi-Fi+ settings.
  • Touch the Wi-Fi+ switch to enable Wi-Fi+.

Done.

Voice wakeup

If you can't find your phone when you know it's somewhere nearby, or your hands are tied when you need to call somebody, use voice wakeup to find your phone and place calls.

  • On the home screen, touch Settings.
  • Touch Voice wakeup.
  • Enable Voice wakeup.
  • Touch Wakeup keyword type(we'll set up a custom keyword in this example).
  • Touch Custom wakeup keyword.
  • Enter your custom keyword.
  • Touch the check mark.
  • Begin recording your keyword.
  • Touch the check mark.
  • When your screen is locked, say your keyword to find your phone or use voice dialing.

Done.

Dual windows

Dual windows offers you more flexibility to interact with your device by giving you an active split-screen view.

  • Touch and hold the recent key (or swipe up using two fingers) to enable dual windows.
  • Open two different applications in the upper and lower windows.
  • Touch the action dot in the center for more options.
  • • Touch the swap icon to swap the positions of the applications.
  • • Touch the home icon to return to the dual windows home screen.
  • • Touch the close icon to close dual windows.

Done.


You can enable or disable the dual windows feature from Settings > Dual windows.
